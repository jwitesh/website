About Us

BUGS organizes workshops, seminars, and training sessions that cover a range of topics, from technical skills to leadership qualities. These programs are designed to equip students with practical knowledge and skills that are essential for personal and professional growth.

Our Mission

BUGS embodies the spirit of student empowerment at LPU. By providing a platform for creativity, collaboration, and leadership, this community is making a significant impact on the personal and professional development of its members. As BUGS continues to grow and evolve, it remains dedicated to helping students unlock their full potential and achieve their goals.

Our Vision

Joining BUGS is an opportunity for any LPU student who is passionate about making a difference and eager to be part of a collaborative community. Whether you're interested in leading a project, participating in workshops, or simply exploring new ideas, BUGS welcomes students who are enthusiastic about learning and contributing.

What we do?

1.Leadership Development: Through workshops, seminars, and mentorship programs, we helpstudents develop essential leadership skills and build a strong professional network.
2. Skill Building: From resume workshops to public speaking courses, we offer practical resources to enhance student's personal and professional skills.
3. Community Engagement: We believe in the power of collaboration and community. Our events and projects encourage students to work together, share ideas, and support each other’s goals.
4. Guidance: Our club guides career planning, internships, and job searching, ensuring that students are well-prepared for their future endeavours.
5. Opportunities: BUGS connects students with industry professionals, alumni, and fellow students, creating valuable networking opportunities that can open doors and spark new possibilities.
